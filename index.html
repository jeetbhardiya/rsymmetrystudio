About the Designer

Rutvi
Rakholiya

Interior Designer · Ahmedabad

With a deep love for materials, light, and the quiet power of thoughtfully arranged space, Rutvi brings a poetic sensibility to every home she touches.

Her work blends minimalist restraint with warmth — creating homes that feel deeply personal, unhurried, and beautifully liveable. Every project begins with listening, and ends with a space that feels as though it could not have existed any other way.

Design Philosophy

Beauty lives in the balance between restraint and warmth.

Rutvi believes that great design is not about excess, but about editing — knowing what to remove as much as what to add. Each space she creates is a quiet statement of refined taste and deep intentionality.
